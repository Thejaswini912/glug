Events

Online Webathon

It was a virtual event conducted by GLUG-PESCE during the period of lock down.Event started on 26th March at 6:00pm and ended on 27th March at 10:00pm where students were given 28 hours to design static website for study on the current CORONA outbreak.All the intrested students 11 in number witnessed the competition.Theme was announced 10 minutes prior to the competition. The judges for the competition were B.N.Ruchika, M.Adil and Athithya. Winners were awarded with cash prize.

HTML5 and CSS3

The event was held on 8th Feb 2020.The event started at 2:30 pm with 107 participants.Basic terms of web like http,www,internet was dealt first, followed by basics of html,various tags and their attributes were taught.A simple website had to be made by participants. Extension of the same event i.e. CSS was done on 7th march 2020,from 1:30pm-4:30pm with types of CSS selectors and box Model being major topics

Vector Designing

The Vector Designing Workshop was held on 15.02.2020 at Computer Science and Engineering Seminar Hall at P.E.S College of Engineering, Mandya by GNU/Linux Users Group PESCE.Inkscape was used as a software for vector designing along with the many tools of inkscape, various types of image formats were also discussed

GLUG-o-week

It was a mega event for a week called GLUG-O-WEEK. The sessions of the event were namely, 1. Design Web App + Git: It was conducted on 30th and 31st of October 2019, where members were taught to design a web application using python and it's framework called flask. 2.Dexterous + Localisation: These two activities were online, conducted on 3rd November and onwards and Dexterous was an online poster making competition which happened successfully for a day.

Open Street Mapping

FSMK team started the session and they spoke about what exactly is OSM, why mapping is required and important. Then they made to install F: Droid, OSM and++, GSM tracker.Students started learning how to map under the guidance of FSMK team and GLUG-PESCE volunteers and mapped important locations

GIMP

On 19th October,2019 GLUG-PESCE organised an Workshop on GIMP, a free designing software to help students understand the advantages and the diversity of tools in GIMP. Speakers Yeshwanth and Tanusha taught about basic commands and tools for designing a poster, editing a picture and creating a new project. There were more than 100 participants from different departments.

Shell Scripting

An event on shell scripting was conducted on 21st Feb 2019 by GLUG-PESCE to help people understand some of the basics of shell script programming (aka shell scripting), and to introduce some of the possibilities of simple but powerful programming available under the Bourne shell.

Unbox The Buzz

GLUG – PESCE conducted a session called UNBOX THE BUZZ on 22nd September 2018 . This Session was on a technical talk of some of the new buzz words around us. The core members of the GLUG-PESCE were given a chance to choose one of these buzz words and give a presentation on it for 10 to 15 minutes.

Essay Writing

Julian Assange, founder of wiki leaks in 2006 was arrested on 11th april 2019.GLUG-PESCE conducted an online essay writing contest on 24th april 2019 and the topic was "Why should Julian Assange be released ?" with 500 being the word limit.Students from others colleges also participated

Localisation

GLUG-PESCE took an initiative to translate Wikipedia articles to Kannada. The members and the volunteers were divided into 8 teams where all the team had atleast one GLUG core member to monitor the work . The topics were chosen in such a way that it relates to the Free Software Movement

Womens' Day Celebration

This event was celebrated on the occasion of womens’ day. This was team event of maximum 4 in a team in which 2 of them should be girls. In this event the teams have to market the item given to them. The items were hidden in the surroundings of the ISE department in PESCE where the event was scheduled. The clues were written in chits and was randomly given to the teams .

Livoss

LiVOSS stands for live boot virtualization operating system shell scripting.Prior to this event all GLUG-CORE members prepared for live booting.This event was successful with total attendees exceeding 75 members.
