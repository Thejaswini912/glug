About

GLUG

GLUG (GNU/Linux Users Group) is a socio-technical community which urges for Free Education and Free Society. Today GLUGs are huge communities all around the world, the ideology of GNU/Linux Users Group is the brainchild of Free Software Movement.


Free Software Movement

Free Software Movement was started in 1983 by a Free Software enthusiast named Dr.Richard M Stallman by launching the GNU project. The main objective of the Free Software Movement is to provide 'Software Freedom' for the users.


Free Software Movement Karnataka

Free Software Movement Karnataka is a virtual body of Free Software Movement, resided in Bangaluru serving the purpose. FSMK is associated with many colleges and community centres which are located in various parts of the state, helping to spreading the word of Free Software.


GLUG-PESCE

GLUG-PESCE is the virtual body of FSMK, started in the year 2012, to spread the word of Free Software in Mandya city. GLUG-PESCE has got many active members, who are working towards the goals of Free Software.
GLUG is active from the year 2012 and it is celebrating 8th anniversary this August.

Total number of GLUG-PESCE members in the academic year 2019-20 are 114 and in 2018-19 was 150.
